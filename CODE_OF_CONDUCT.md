# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering an open, inclusive, respectful, and professional environment for everyone participating in this project, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

This repository focuses on **quantum cryptography, security, and scientific research**, and therefore requires a particularly high standard of professional and ethical conduct.

---

## Expected Behavior

All contributors and participants are expected to:

- Engage respectfully in discussions and reviews
- Use inclusive, professional, and constructive language
- Respect differing scientific opinions and interpretations
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Prioritize correctness, reproducibility, and clarity

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment, discrimination, or offensive comments
- Personal attacks or hostile communication
- Misrepresentation of research results or plagiarism
- Publishing private or sensitive information without consent
- Any behavior that undermines scientific integrity

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Discussions and comments
- External communication representing the project

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to take appropriate action in response to violations, including:

- Issuing warnings
- Removing comments or contributions
- Temporarily or permanently restricting participation

---
